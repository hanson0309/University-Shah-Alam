Master in Computer Science

About the Course

The Master in Computer Science programme at Management and Science University is a comprehensive and professionally oriented computer science module which blends the basics of computer science with contemporary and applied skills imperative to a career in technology. This programme is well-suited to individuals with an interest in specific technology related areas from a range that includes computer engineering, mobile computing, and data analytics. Through the course of this programme, you will collaborate with accomplished lecturers experienced in industry and work with colleagues of a similar mind-set. Upon graduation, you will be well-equipped with the skillset needed in this industry.


Entry Requirements

(i) A Bachelor's Degree in Computing field with CGPA of 2.5 and above. Candidate with a CGPA below 2.5 but above 2.0 may be accepted, subject to rigorous internal review process;


OR


(ii) Other equivalent qualification recognised by Malaysian Government.


Duration of Study

1 - 6 years


Fees

RM15,500
